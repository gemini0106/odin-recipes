Ampesi

Plantain Ampesi With Stew, Salted Fish, Egg and Pear

Ampesi is a traditional meal in the Ghanaian cuisine and is prepared with boiled yam, boiled plantain, boiled cocoyam or boiled cassava or a mix of these and is served with a spicy gravy or vegetable stew or kontomire or thick palm-nut soup.

This food is very common among the akans of Ghana . I love it because it's easy to prepare and very nutritious . It contain all the vital nutrients you will need to run the day.
